J.5.17 Math error reporting
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. _9899_J.5.17p1:

:ref:`1 <9899_J.5.17p1>` Functions declared in :ref:`\<complex.h> <9899_7.3>` and :ref:`\<math.h> <9899_7.12>` raise SIGFPE to report errors instead of, or in addition to, setting errno or raising floating-point exceptions (:ref:`7.3 <9899_7.3>`, :ref:`7.12 <9899_7.12>`).
