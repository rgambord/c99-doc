.. rubric:: Description

.. _9899_7.3.9.3p2:

.. container:: snum

   :ref:`2 <9899_7.3.9.3p2>`

The conj functions compute the complex conjugate of z, by reversing the sign of its imaginary part.

.. rubric:: Returns

.. _9899_7.3.9.3p3:

.. container:: snum

   :ref:`3 <9899_7.3.9.3p3>`

The conj functions return the complex conjugate value.
