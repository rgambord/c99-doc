J.5.6 Other arithmetic types
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. _9899_J.5.6p1:

.. container:: snum

   :ref:`1 <9899_J.5.6p1>`

Additional arithmetic types, such as \__int128 or double double, and their appropriate conversions are defined (:ref:`6.2.5 <9899_6.2.5>`, :ref:`6.3.1 <9899_6.3.1>`). Additional floating types may have more range or precision than long double, may be used for evaluating expressions of other floating types, and may be used to define float_t or double_t.
