.. container:: snum

   :ref:`1 <9899_3p1>`

For the purposes of this International Standard, the following
definitions apply. Other terms are defined where they appear in italic type or
on the left side of a syntax rule. Terms explicitly defined in this
International Standard are not to be presumed to refer implicitly to similar
terms defined elsewhere. Terms not defined in this International Standard are
to be interpreted according to ISO/IEC 2382-1. Mathematical symbols not defined
in this International Standard are to be interpreted according to ISO 31-11.
